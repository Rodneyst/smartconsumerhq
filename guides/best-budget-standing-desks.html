Best Budget Standing Desks Under $400 (2026)

We tested 10 electric standing desks under $400 — evaluating motor noise, stability, assembly quality, and long-term durability.

🕐 8 min read 🔬 Independently Tested 📅 Updated June 2026

Standing desks have come down dramatically in price over the past few years, but the cheap end of the market is still full of unstable frames, noisy motors, and misleading weight capacity claims. We spent four weeks testing 10 desks priced under $400 to find the ones worth buying.

What We Tested

Each desk was assembled, used daily for at least five days, and evaluated on motor smoothness, wobble at standing height, controller quality, cable management options, and assembly time. We also stress-tested each motor by running full height cycles 50 times.
