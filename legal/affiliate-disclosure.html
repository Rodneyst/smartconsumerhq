Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: June 2026

Smart Consumer HQ participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means we may earn a commission when you click on certain links on our site and make a qualifying purchase — at no additional cost to you.

How We Use Affiliate Links

Some of the product links on our buying guides, reviews, and category pages are affiliate links. These links are clearly embedded within our content and point to retailers such as Amazon, and other relevant merchants. When you purchase through one of these links, we receive a small commission.

Our Editorial Independence

Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial decisions. We recommend products based on independent research, testing, and analysis. We do not accept payment for positive reviews or guaranteed placements. If a product doesn't make the cut, it doesn't appear in our guides — regardless of whether an affiliate relationship exists.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, Smart Consumer HQ discloses its affiliate relationships on relevant pages. This disclosure page serves as a site-wide notice of our participation in affiliate programs.
