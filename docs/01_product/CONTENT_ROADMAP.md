## Prioritization Logic

### Why these 20 guides, in this order

**Guides 1–3 (Existing placeholders):** These pages are already indexed by Google. Publishing full content on indexed pages drives rankings faster than launching new URLs. Priority 1–3 regardless of niche.

**Guide 4 (Cashback Apps):** Fills the empty Money Saving category. High search volume, strong CPC (advertisers pay well for financial-intent keywords). Cashback apps pay affiliate commissions per install — not dependent on Amazon Associates approval.

**Guide 5 (Robot Vacuums):** Fills the empty Home Products category. Robot vacuums are the highest-commission home product on Amazon Associates (average order $250–400). Strong recurring demand.

**Guides 6–8:** Three of the highest-volume, highest-CPC buying guide niches across the site's existing categories. Adjustable dumbbells, ergonomic chairs, and wireless earbuds consistently appear in affiliate site earnings reports as top performers.

**Guides 9–20:** Systematic category build-out. Each fills a strategic niche, adds internal linking depth, and expands the topical authority footprint.

## Internal Linking Plan

Every guide links to:
1. Its parent category page (e.g., home-office.html)
2. At least 1 related guide in the same category
3. Homepage

Category pages link to all published guides in that category.

## Content Update Schedule

Once live, buying guides should be reviewed and updated:
