# Monetization Plan — Smart Consumer HQ

**Created:** 2026-06-09  
**Last updated:** 2026-06-09

This document outlines every affiliate program and revenue channel planned for Smart Consumer HQ, the milestones required before applying, expected commission rates, and the recommended activation sequence.

---

## Revenue Model Summary

Smart Consumer HQ earns revenue when readers click affiliate links in buying guides and complete a purchase. The site never charges readers; commissions come from the merchant.

**Revenue funnel:**  
Organic search → buying guide → affiliate product link → purchase → commission

**Target timeline to first revenue:** 90–120 days from first complete guide.

## Affiliate Program Profiles

### 1. Amazon Associates

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Program** | Amazon Associates |
| **URL** | affiliate-program.amazon.com |
| **Commission Rate** | 1–10% depending on category (4% Home & Kitchen, 6% Home Improvement, 3% Computers, 3% Electronics) |
| **Cookie Duration** | 24 hours (add-to-cart: 90 days) |
| **Payment Threshold** | $10 (gift card), $100 (check/direct deposit) |
| **Payment Schedule** | 60 days after end of month earned |
| **Monetization Score** | ★★★★★ — highest priority; covers the majority of products on the site |
| **Status** | Not yet applied |

**Why it's the primary program:** Amazon has the widest product catalog, highest consumer trust, and the best conversion rates. Even at lower commission percentages, the volume and trust factor makes it the #1 affiliate network for physical product reviews.

**Products covered:** Fitness equipment, office chairs, standing desks, smartwatches, earbuds, home products, baby gear, car seats, tech gadgets.

**Milestones required before applying:**
- [ ] 3 complete, well-written buying guides published (currently: 1/3)
- [ ] Site live and deployed on custom domain ✓
- [ ] Privacy policy page live ✓
- [ ] Affiliate disclosure page live ✓
- [ ] All affiliate links must go live within 180 days of approval (requirement to avoid rejection)
- [ ] Application: apply at affiliate-program.amazon.com → account setup → website URL review

**Current blocker:** Only 1 complete guide published. Write `best-budget-standing-desks.html` and `best-smartwatches-under-300.html` to reach the minimum.

## Important Rules

- **Never add affiliate links before Amazon Associates approval** — Amazon can reject or ban accounts that link before approval
- **Always disclose affiliate relationships** — Affiliate disclosure banner required on every guide page; `/legal/affiliate-disclosure` must remain live
- **FTC compliance** — All sponsored/affiliate links must have `rel="sponsored"` and visible disclosure per FTC guidelines
- **Link rot monitoring** — Check affiliate links every 90 days; Amazon product pages disappear regularly
- **Cookie stacking** — Never use cookie-stuffing techniques; stick to standard text/button links
