## Content Quality Standards

Every guide published on SmartConsumerHQ must meet:

**Minimum requirements:**
- 3,000+ words (target: 3,500–5,500)
- 5 product reviews with pros/cons
- Quick picks table and full comparison table
- Buying guide section (what to look for, minimum 4 factors)
- Common mistakes section (minimum 4 items)
- FAQ accordion with 6+ questions
- FAQPage + Article JSON-LD schema
- Affiliate disclosure above fold
- All affiliate links: `rel="nofollow sponsored" target="_blank"`
- Internal links to all existing guides in related categories
- Link to parent category page

**SEO requirements:**
- Title tag: target keyword + year + site name, under 60 characters
- Meta description: benefit-focused, under 160 characters
- Canonical: extension-free URL
- H1 matches title intent
- Target keyword in H1, first paragraph, and at least one H2
